Privacy Policy for LifeTime Pomodoro

1. Introduction

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application LifeTime Pomodoro for Windows devices. The application is a productivity tool (Pomodoro timer) designed to help users manage their time.

2. Data Collection and Usage

The Application does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data, user information, or telemetry. All settings, timers, and statistics (if any) are stored locally on your device and are not accessible by the developer or any third parties.

3. Third-Party Access

Since no data is collected, no data is shared with third-party services.

4. Permissions

The application may require standard Windows permissions to function correctly (e.g., notifications for timer alerts), but these permissions are used strictly for the app's functionality.
